Nothing is too hard for God. The very existence of Israel is an example of the mighty power of God. How could Gideon defeat the armies of the Midianites and the Amalekites with just 300 men?

With God all things are possible. "Thus Midian was subdued before the Israelites and did not raise its head again. During Gideon's lifetime, the land had peace forty years" (Judges 8:28) How could a tiny nation of 600,000 Jews defeat the armies of five nations with combined populations of over 100 million? With God all things are possible.
